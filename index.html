About

VoziMe! Is an idea of an application whose primary audience would be young people who use smartphones in their everyday lives, as well as all those who can navigate through a simple application. The goal is to make taxis as accessible as possible, so that we do not have to call several phone numbers to get a vehicle to pick us up. Enjoy!
